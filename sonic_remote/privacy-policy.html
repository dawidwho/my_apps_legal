Privacy Policy — Sonic Remote Control

Last updated: May 27, 2025

Overview

Sonic Remote Control ("the app") is developed by Dawid Who.

This privacy policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

The app does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information, usage data, analytics, or any other data about you or your device to any server or third party.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Names you give to your saved remotes
  • Device type, brand, and IR profile selections for each saved remote

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

IR Database

The app includes a local database of IR (infrared) codes used to control devices. This database is stored within the app itself and does not require an internet connection.

Permissions

The app requests the following permission:

TRANSMIT_IR — Required to send infrared signals through your phone's IR blaster to control devices. This permission is used solely for its stated purpose.

Internet Access

The app does not require or use internet access. No network requests are made.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated policy will be made available at the same URL and the "last updated" date above will be revised.
